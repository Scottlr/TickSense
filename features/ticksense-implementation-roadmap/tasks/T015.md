### T015 - Araxxor Strategy and Spider Opportunities

#### Goal
Add post-MVP Araxxor spider engagement, boss re-engagement, damage, and finish-reason analytics from verified normalized telemetry.

#### Scope
- Create `AraxxorStrategy`, `AraxxorState`, `SpiderOpportunityTracker`, and `AraxxorActivityData`.
- Activate only from verified Araxxor NPC presence, instance/region evidence, and player interaction.
- Emit spider engagement, spider death to boss re-engage, and boss target lost opportunities.
- Attribute damage during boss opportunity windows using local-player hitsplats.
- Terminate on boss death/despawn with evidence, loot/reward evidence, player death, teleport, leave instance/region, logout/hop, or boss absent plus idle timeout.

#### Out of scope
- No Vardorvis/Inferno mechanics.
- No live target overlays, warnings, sounds, or prompt text.
- No unverified projectile/graphic claims.
- No alternate boss strategy.
- No MVP registration; this is post-MVP and must not block the first release.

#### Misinterpretations To Prevent
- Wrong interpretation: showing a live spider status panel is acceptable because the strategy detects mechanics.
  Correct rule: spider detection is for post-kill reporting only.
- Wrong interpretation: any damage during a fight belongs to a spider opportunity.
  Correct rule: damage attribution requires the spider opportunity/span to be open.

#### Invariants
- Boss strategies consume only normalized telemetry.
- Every boss activity emits a finish reason with evidence and confidence.
- Unknown or low-confidence mechanics stay out of normal reports.
- Araxxor is post-MVP and is enabled for normal reports only when verified.
