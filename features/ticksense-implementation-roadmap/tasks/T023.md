### T023 - Gem Mining Registry and Verification Fixtures

#### Goal
Verify the RuneLite-visible evidence needed for gem mining and create source-owned primitive ID registries and fixtures.

#### Scope
- Create `GemMiningIds` with primitive object, region, animation, and item IDs plus source comments.
- Add capture instructions and anonymized normalized fixture placeholders for `gem-mining-basic.jsonl`.
- Verify rock availability, mine click evidence, mining animation or XP/inventory confirmation, and rock depletion/respawn evidence.

#### Out of scope
- No gem mining strategy, analyzer, or UI changes.
- No live rock highlights or click suggestions.
- No arbitrary user-defined IDs.
