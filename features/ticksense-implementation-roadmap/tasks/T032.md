### T032 - Construction Strategy and Opportunities

#### Goal
Detect one verified Construction method and its menu/cadence opportunities using observe-only telemetry.

#### Scope
- Create `ConstructionStrategy` and `ConstructionState`.
- Emit `CONSTRUCTION_MENU_LATENCY`, `CONSTRUCTION_BUILD_REMOVE_CADENCE`, `CONSTRUCTION_INVENTORY_CYCLE`, and `CONSTRUCTION_BANKING_DOWNTIME`.
