### T034 - Vardorvis Strategy and Opportunities

#### Goal
Detect verified Vardorvis mechanic opportunities from normalized telemetry without live solving.

#### Scope
- Create `VardorvisStrategy` and `VardorvisState`.
- Emit only verified mechanic opportunities such as ranged-head response, blood-splat movement, axe dodge, and prayer response.
- Attribute damage during verified mechanic windows.

#### Out of scope
